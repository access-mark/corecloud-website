About CoreCloud

Executive Overview

Founded by seasoned tech executive Mark Louw, CoreCloud (Pty) Ltd is a South African technology group built to simplify enterprise IT while driving scalable digital transformation. With decades of experience leading sales, partnerships, and infrastructure at global firms like NTT, Dell EMC, and TrueMark, Mark’s vision for CoreCloud is simple — deliver trusted, results-driven solutions with elite service and African pride.

From infrastructure and cybersecurity to workforce enablement and AI, CoreCloud exists to help businesses modernize with confidence, speed, and measurable ROI.

Our Mission & Values

CoreCloud is more than a service provider — we are a strategic partner. Our mission is to be the engine room behind Africa’s most ambitious organizations. We lead with expertise, execute with precision, and operate with the highest standards of accountability.

Our values are simple: Excellence. Integrity. Execution. We don’t just consult — we deliver.

Group Structure

CoreCloud operates as a group of focused technology divisions, each engineered to solve a critical need:

  • Access Zero: Cybersecurity and digital infrastructure
  • CoreTech: Hybrid IT and managed infrastructure
  • CoreCapacity: Talent placement and workforce augmentation
  • CoreAI: Automation, AI, and data science solutions
  • CoreFin: Financial services and OPEX enablement (coming soon)
