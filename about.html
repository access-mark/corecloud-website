About CoreCloud

Executive Overview

CoreCloud was founded by cloud strategist Mark Louw to address Africa’s unique enterprise challenges. With leadership roles at NTT, Dell EMC & TrueMark, Mark’s vision is to deliver trusted, outcome-driven IT solutions that fuel growth.

From secure infrastructure to AI-powered decision support, we exist to help organizations modernize with confidence and measurable ROI.

Mission & Values

We’re more than a technology provider—we’re your strategic partner. We lead with integrity, execute with excellence, and deliver real-world impact.

  • Excellence. World-class execution, every time.
  • Integrity. Transparent, trust-based partnerships.
  • Accountability. We own outcomes, not just outputs.
