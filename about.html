About CoreCloud

Executive Overview

Founded by cloud strategist Mark Louw, CoreCloud is South Africa’s premier broker of enterprise-scale IT outcomes. Drawing on decades of leadership at NTT, Dell EMC, and TrueMark, we deliver trusted solutions that drive measurable growth.

From secure infrastructure to AI-driven insights, our mission is simple: empower African enterprises to compete globally.
