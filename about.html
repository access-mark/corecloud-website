About CoreCloud

Executive Overview

CoreCloud was founded by visionary strategist Mark Louw to address Africa’s most pressing enterprise IT challenges. With senior leadership experience at NTT, Dell EMC, and TrueMark, Mark brings a deep understanding of cloud, infrastructure, and transformation to the South African market.

Whether deploying secure cloud environments, augmenting delivery teams, or optimizing infrastructure, CoreCloud drives measurable outcomes — with precision, pace, and purpose.

Mission & Values

Our mission is bold but simple — to be the engine room for Africa’s modern enterprise. We lead engagements with strategic intent, operational precision, and relentless execution.

  • Excellence: Execution without compromise.
  • Integrity: Transparent partnerships built on trust.
  • Accountability: We own delivery. We own impact.

Group Structure

  • Access Zero: Cybersecurity & digital infrastructure.
  • CoreTech: Hybrid IT & managed services.
  • CoreCapacity: Talent placement & delivery teams.
  • CoreAI: Automation, data science & applied AI.
  • CoreFin: Tech enablement & OPEX financing.
  • CoreDev: Strategic software development partners.

Meet the Founder

Mark Louw, Founder & CEO, is a results-focused operator with deep experience building revenue-generating IT ecosystems across Africa. He is relentless in delivery — and committed to empowering African enterprises to scale, secure, and lead.
