About CoreCloud

Executive Overview

CoreCloud was founded by tech visionary Mark Louw with a clear mandate: simplify enterprise IT while delivering measurable business outcomes.

Leveraging decades of leadership at global firms like Dell EMC, NTT and TrueMark, Mark built CoreCloud to bridge strategy and execution—ensuring every solution scales, secures, and accelerates growth.

Our Mission & Values

We exist to empower African enterprises with modern, intelligent IT.

  • Excellence: We uphold the highest standards in everything we do.
  • Integrity: Transparent, accountable partnerships you can trust.
  • Execution: We deliver on time, on budget, and on promise.

Group Structure

CoreCloud is structured into four specialist divisions, each engineered to solve a critical enterprise challenge:

CoreTech: Hybrid infrastructure architects—designing and managing resilient, performant networks and data centers.

CoreCapacity: Elite talent placement—building on-demand teams of certified engineers, developers, and data scientists.

CoreAI: Data & AI platforms—delivering custom automation, analytics, and machine-learning solutions.

CoreFin: OPEX financing—enabling flexible consumption models and partner onboarding support.
